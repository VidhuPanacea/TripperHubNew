Himachal With Golden Temple

10 Days 09 Nights
4 - 12

Itinerary


  • Day 1: Delhi – Shimla ( 350 Kms )

    Upon arrival in Delhi, our representative will meet you and transfer to Shimla. Arrive Shimla by evening, check in at hotel, evening at leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 2: Shimla ( Local Shimla )

    Morning after breakfast proceed to Shimla sightseeing like Kufri, Jakhoo Hills, Voisroi Lodge, Indira Gandhi Tourist Park, Mini Zoo, Chini Bunglow. Evening free to stroll in the famous shopping place of Shimla town – The Mall & The Ridge. Dinner and Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 3: Shimla - Kullu - Manali (260 Kms) 9 Hrs.

    Morning after breakfast proceed to Manali. Enroute visit kullu, Kullu Valley, Shawl Factory, Sunder Nagar Lake, Hanogi Mata Temple & Pandoh Dam sightseeing . By evening you will reach to Manali, check in at your prebooked Hotel . Dinner & Overnight stay at hotel in Manali.

  • Day 4: Manali (Local Manali).

    Morning after breakfast proceed to full day local Manali sightseeing like Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, Tibetan Monastery and Vashisht Village. Evening free to visit Mall Road .Back to Hotel. Dinner & Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 5: Manali (Solang Valley/Gulaba)

    Morning after breakfast proceed to full day Kothi, George , Gulaba , Marhi , Or Solang Valley. Dinner & Overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 6: Manali – Dharamshala ( 240 Kms )

    Morning after breakfast proceed to Dharamshala and Check in Hotel and after taking some rest proceed to local Dharamshala sightseeing like Lord Eligin’s Memorial, Dal lake, Bhagshunath Temple and Water Fall, Dalai Lama Temple, Cricket Stadium. Dinner and Overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 7: Dharamshala – Dalhousie ( 140 Kms )

    Morning after breakfast proceeds to Dalhousie. Check in the hotel and after sometime proceed to local sightseeing of Dalhousie like Churches, Panchpula, Subhash Baoli, Bara Pather etc.. Dinner & overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 8: Dalhousie ( Khajjiar – Kalatop )

    Morning after breakfast proceed to full day sightseeing of Khajjiar,& Kalatop sightseeing. Dinner and Overnight stay at Hotel.​

  • Day 9: Dalhousie – Amritsar ( 190 Kms )

    Morning after breakfast proceeds to Amritsar. Check in the hotel and after taking some rest proceed to local Amritsar sightseeing like Golden Temple, Jallianwala bagh, Wagah Border etc. Dinner & Overnight stay at Hotel.​

  • Day 10: Amritsar – Delhi ( 480 Kms )

    Morning after breakfast proceeds to Delhi. Drop at Airport/Railway Station for onward journey.​
