Dharamshala Dalhousie Volvo

07 Days 06 Nights
4 - 12

Itinerary


  • Day 1: Delhi – Dharamshala (530 Kms. )

    Get in the Volvo bus from Delhi Dharamshala in the evening and overnight journey in the Volvo bus

  • Day 2: Dharamshala Arrival (Local Dharamshala)

    After getting Dharamshala in the morning check in the hotel and after having some rest proceed to local Dharamshala sightseeing flight ticket stadium tea garden local Dharamshala market etc dinner and overnight stay at hotel

  • Day 3: Dharamshala ( Local Mcleodganj )

    After getting fresh enjoy first meal of morning and start toward full day sightseeing of McLeod Ganj like Dalai Lama temple, monsoon temple, the lake energy waterfall Kangra art museum war memorial sunset swimming pool etc by the evening get back to hotel have delicious dinner and overnight stay

  • Day 4: Dharamshala – Dalhousie ( Local Dalhousie )

    Get a refresh morning Breeze and have some breakfast move toward Dalhousie check in the hotel and after taking some break proceed to local Dalhousie promenade like Subhash bowli, Panj pulla charges dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 5: Dalhousie ( Khajjiar – Kalatop )

    In morning after breakfast full day excursion of cousin and colour top by the evening get back to the hotel have delicious fest and overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 6: Dalhousie – Dharamshala – Delhi (530 Kms. )

    After getting in the morning have first meal of the day check out from hotel and move along to Dharamshala in evening catch the Volvo from Dharamshala Delhi overnight journey by the bus ​
