Himachal Tour package

09 Days 08 Nights
4 - 12

Itinerary


  • Day 1: Delhi – Shimla (350 Kms )

    Upon arrival in Delhi, our representative will meet you and transfer to Shimla. Arrive Shimla by evening, check in at hotel, evening at leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 2: Shimla ( Local Shimla )

    In Morning after breakfast proceed to Shimla sightseeing like Kufri, Jakhoo Hills, Voisroi Lodge, Indira Gandhi Tourist Park, Mini Zoo, Chini Bunglow. Evening free to stroll in the famous shopping place of Shimla town – The Mall & The Ridge. Dinner and Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 3: Shimla - Kullu - Manali (260 Kms) 9 Hrs.

    Morning after breakfast proceed to Manali. Enroute visit kullu, Kullu Valley, Shawl Factory, Sunder Nagar Lake, Hanogi Mata Temple & Pandoh Dam sightseeing . By evening you will reach to Manali, check in at your prebooked Hotel . Dinner & Overnight stay at hotel in Manali.

  • Day 4: Manali ( Local Manali )

    Morning after breakfast proceed to local Manali sightseeing like Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, Tibetan Monastery and Vashisht Village. Evening free to visit Mall Road . Evening Back to Hotel . Dinner & Overnight stay at hote

  • Day 5: Manali (Solang Valley/Gulaba)

    Morning after breakfast proceed to full day Kothi, George , Gulaba , Marhi , Or Solang Valley. Dinner & Overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 6: Manali – Dharamshala ( 240 Kms )

    Morning after breakfast proceed to Dharamshala and Check in Hotel and after taking some rest proceed to local Dharamshala sightseeing like Lord Eligin’s Memorial, Dal lake, Bhagshunath Temple and Water Fall, Dalai Lama Temple, Cricket Stadium. Dinner and Overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 7: Dharamshala – Dalhousie ( 140 Kms )

    Morning after breakfast proceed to Dalhousie. Check in the hotel and after sometime proceed to local sightseeing of Dalhousie like Churches, Panchpula, Subhash Baoli, Bara Pather etc.. Dinner & overnight stay at hotel.​

  • Day 8: Dalhousie ( Chamba – Khajjiar – Kalatop )

    Morning after breakfast proceed to full day sightseeing of Chamba, Khajjiar, Kalatop sightseeing. Dinner and Overnight stay at Hotel.​

  • Day 9: Dalhousie – Delhi ( 580 Kms )

    Morning after breakfast proceeds to Delhi. Drop at airport, bus stand railway station with onward journey...​
