Itinerary


  • Day 1: Delhi – Manali (570 Kms. )

    Get in the Volvo bus from New Delhi to Manali in the evening around 5:30 p.m. overnight journey in the Volvo bus.

  • Day 2: Manali (Manali Local Sightseeing)

    On getting destination a day down check in the hotel after taking taking some rest proceed to local Manali mesmerizing view like hidimba Devi temple Manu temple Vashisht village clubhouse monasteries in evening so let Mall road and enjoy dinner and have restful night.

  • Day 3: Manali (Solang Valley/Gulaba)

    After getting fresh enjoy first meal of morning and proceed to full day kothi girls gulaba murree or solang valley is the most visited to respond in evening back to hotel enjoy delicious fest and overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 4: Manali (Kullu – Manikaran )

    After getting fresh, have a short meal and make a star towards Kullu valley kasol valley rabbit form shawl factory manikaran Gurudwara at the day dawn stroll at local market enjoy dinner and overnight stay at hotel

  • Day 5: Manali – Shimla ( 250 Kms.)

    After having morning first model move forward to Shimla by deluxe bus arrive Shimla by evening and check in hotel evening free to visit Mall road raise Church like your bazar dinner and overnight stay at Shimla.

  • Day 6: Shimla ( Shimla, Kufri Sightseeing )

    Get refresh global traditional climate and proceed to proceed to full day sightseeing of green valley mini zoo chini bungalow for go valley kufri Indira Gandhi tourist park Fun World at evening catch the Volvo for onward journey to Delhi. ​

  • Day 7: Shimla – Delhi ( 350 Kms. )

    In morning we will drop you at New Delhi for onward journey.
